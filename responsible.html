üö® WARNING SIGNS OF GAMBLING ADDICTION

Gambling becomes a problem when it causes harm to you or others. Watch for these signs:

  • Spending more money or time gambling than you can afford
  • Having trouble controlling or stopping gambling behavior
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to gamble
  • Feeling restless or irritable when trying to stop
  • Gambling to escape problems or negative feelings
  • Chasing losses by gambling more to win back money
  • Neglecting work, family, or personal responsibilities
  • Relationship problems caused by gambling
  • Financial difficulties due to gambling losses
